## アクティビティ図

[詳しくは](https://plantuml.com/ja/activity-diagram-beta)

````
```plantuml
start

if (Graphviz installed?) then (yes)
  :process all\ndiagrams;
else (no)
  :process only
  __sequence__ and __activity__ diagrams;
endif

stop
```
````
